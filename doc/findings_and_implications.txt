What are the main findings?

• Random Forest and XGBoost outperformed LSTM in monthly streamflow prediction.
• Random Forest shows the most robust generalization on test data.
• The dominance of lagged precipitation and streamflow reflects basin memory effects.

What are the implications of the main findings?
    • Tree-based ML models appeared to be most suitable for monthly streamflow forecasting in data-limited basins.
    • Explainable AI validates the physical relevance of main hydro-climatic determinants of streamflow.
    • Reliable ML-based forecasts can support national water management in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
